09 Questions

Before you join.

When does Made to Market launch?+

Launch dates will be announced to the waitlist first. Members will also get the first opportunity to join the founding intake.

Do I need professional experience?+

No. The programme is designed for ambitious beginners as well as creators and social media managers who want stronger commercial skills.

Is this only about Instagram?+

No. You will learn the thinking that sits underneath strong content, then how to adapt the work for different platforms, formats and client goals.

Will the programme help me get clients?+

Client packaging, pitching and delivery are part of the programme. No course can guarantee clients, but you will leave with a clearer offer and a repeatable approach to finding and serving them.
